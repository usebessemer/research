## Voice

Match the register in Stu's AIOS `references/voice.md` (`/Users/stupeters187/AI-dev/AI-OS/references/voice.md`). Key rules:

- No em dashes. Hyphens, colons, and semicolons are fine.
- Sentence length is variable. Longer when there is nuance to deliver. Short when logistical.
- Prose, not bullets, when drafting external-facing copy. Bullets fine in operational or planning content.
- Polite frame, candid middle.
- Never publish or push as Stu without showing him a draft first.
